Our Story

Fruitify World was started with a dream to bring healthy, organic, and sustainable fruits to every household. Our journey began in Gujarat with a small team and big dreams — now we work with over 500 farmers across India.

We make it easy for you to eat better while helping the planet and our farming communities.

Our Vision & Mission

🌍 Vision

To become the most trusted fruit delivery brand that cares for people and planet equally.

🌿 Mission

Deliver organic fruits directly from farms, support rural employment, and reduce food waste.

Our Journey

2020

Started with 5 farmers and 1 city — Surat, Gujarat.

2021

Added 50+ farmers, started delivery in 4 states.

2022

Partnered with 200+ farmers, crossed 20,000 orders.

2024

Launched app and eco-packaging, expanded pan-India.
